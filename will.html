Luke Skywalker

I was born on the planet Polis Masa, to Padme Amidala and Anakin Skywalker, and grew up on Tatooine.

Obi-Wan Kenobi and Yoda trained me to be a Jedi Knight.

I defeated Darth Vader (my father) and the Emporer on the Second Death Star.

I appear in the following films (in release order):

  1. A New Hope
    • Joined the Rebellion
    • Destroyed the Death Star
  2. The Empire Strikes Back
    • Training by Yoda on Dagobah
    • Fought Darth Vader on Bespin and lost right hand
  3. Return of the Jedi
    • Rescued Han Solo from Jabba the Hutt
    • Defeated the Emporer and Darth Vader on the Second Death Star
  4. Revenge of the Sith
    • Born on Polis Massa
    • Mother, Padme Amidala, dies in childbirth
  5. The Force Awakens
    • Disappeard to hunt for the original Jedi Temple
    • Tracked down by Rey
